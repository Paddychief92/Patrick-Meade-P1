Our History

Established in the year 2000, Bralca has become a cornerstone in the storage and distribution of frozen and chilled food across Ireland. Strategically positioned near the M7, M9, and M50 road networks, facilitating unparalleled reach to our nationwide customers.

Our commitment to excellence is underscored by our full approval from the Department of Agriculture. Over the years, we have dedicated ourselves meeting and exceeding the expectations of our clients, ensuring their products are handled with care and professionalism.

Vision & Mission

At Bralca Cold Store Ltd., our core values are rooted in integrity, quality, and personalized service. We believe in maintaining the highest standards of operational excellence, which is demonstrated by our adherence to the rigorous guidelines set forth by the BRC and the Department of Agriculture, Food, and the Marine. Our strategic location near key transport networks exemplifies our commitment to efficiency and accessibility, ensuring that we can serve our clients’ needs swiftly and effectively.

At the heart of our operations is a dedication to providing tailored solutions that respect the individual needs of each client, emphasizing our belief in building strong, lasting relationships. These values guide us as we continue to deliver exceptional service and support to the frozen and chilled food sectors.
